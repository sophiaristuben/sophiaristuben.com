Experience

Girls Rock Math

I spent my summer after my freshman year of college working for Girls Rock Math, a math camp for 5-11 year old girls where they learn math and do math related art projects. My role was as a Site Director, where I supervised recess, worked one on one with struggling kids, made sure all the materials were ready for each camp day, and on occassion subsituted for the camp teacher. As a woman in STEM myself, it was exciting to see so many young girls already have a passion for math, and I loved getting to be a role model for our high school volunteer counselors as well.

Introduction to CS: Teacher's Assistant

This semester I am working as a Teacher's Assistant for Introduction to Computer Science - Python, a course I took last year. As a TA, I attend the labs where I help the students troubleshoot code, hold a weekly mentor hour where students can get help with understanding the material, and assist the professors with grading assignments.

US Census Bureau

Last summer, I worked as an Enumerator for the US Census Bureau. I walked door to door in various neighborhoods in Seattle to collect Census data for those who had not yet filled out the Census survery for the year.

Donor Experience Specialist

Last spring, I volunteered with Bloodworks Northwest as a Donor Experience Specialist. I supervised and assisted the donors after they gave blood, and assured they were safe and healthy.

Dental Assisting

Last summer, I worked as a Dental Assistant at Snoqualmie Family Dentistry. As an assistant, I sterilized tools, cleaned the rooms, welcomed patients, and aided the office manager with paperwork.

Seattle Youth Symhony Orchestra

I was a member of the Seattle Youth Symphony Orhcestra from 2014 to 2020. In SYSO I got the incredible opportunity to play with French Horn players from the Seattle Symphony as well as play amazing music. I am now playing in the Pomona College Orchestra.
